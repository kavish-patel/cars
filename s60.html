VOLVO S-60

The Volvo S60 is offered in a sole T4 Inscription variant.

The new Volvo S60 was launched in India on 20 January, 2021.

The Volvo S60 sources power from a 2.0-litre, four-cylinder, turbo-petrol motor that produces 188bhp and 300Nm of torque. Paired to an eight-speed automatic transmission, power is sent to the front wheels via an eight-speed automatic transmission.

In terms of design, the Volvo S60 gets a revised front bumper, a chequered grille, sleek headlamps with the Thor Hammer design, C-shaped LED tail lights, chrome-tipped exhausts on either side, and 19-inch alloy wheels.

The Volvo S60 has a seating capacity of five occupants.

The Volvo S60 rivals the Mercedes-Benz C-Class, BMW 3 Series, Audi A4, and the Jaguar XE.
