TATA NEXON ELECTRIC

Tata has increased the prices of the Nexon EV by Rs 25,000 across all its variants.

The electric sub-4m SUV is now priced from Rs 14.54 lakh to Rs 17.15 lakh (ex-showroom pan-India).

The carmaker offers the EV in three trims: XM, XZ+, and XZ+ Lux. It’s also available in select Dark variants.

The Nexon EV uses Tata’s Ziptron EV powertrain technology, packed with a 30.2kWh lithium-ion battery supply with a claimed range of 312km. It gets an 8-year standard warranty and an IP67 waterproof battery pack. The electric motor has a performance rating of 129PS and 245Nm. It is about to get an update soon which will add a bigger battery pack for the electric SUV, thereby increasing its claimed range.

It takes 60 minutes to charge the EV from 0-80 percent using a fast DC charger. Whereas, it takes 8 eight hours using a small home box wall charger to charge the electric SUV from 20 to 80 percent.

Features onboard include a semi-digital instrument cluster (with a 7-inch TFT display), auto AC, auto headlights, a sunroof, a 7-inch touchscreen infotainment system, and connected car technology.

Safety equipment comprises dual front airbags, cornering stability control, and ISOFIX child seat anchors.

It currently doesn’t have any direct rival in its space, though it will take on the upcoming Mahindra XUV300 EV and MG’s sub-Rs 15 lakh EV.
