SKODA OCTAVIA

Skoda has launched the Octavia from Rs 25.99 lakh (ex-showroom) in the country. The sedan is offered with a sole 2.0-litre turbo-petrol engine in two trims: Style and Laurin & Klement.

The Octavia is priced from Rs 25.99 lakh to Rs 28.99 lakh (ex-showroom). It is an alternative to the Hyundai Elantra.

Skoda offers the fourth-gen Octavia with a sole 2.0-litre turbo-petrol engine that produces 190PS and 320Nm. It comes paired only with a 7-speed DSG and a front-wheel drivetrain. The sedan has a claimed efficiency of 15.81kmpl. The Octavia is 4,689mm long, 2,003mm wide, 1,469mm tall, and sits on a 2,680mm wheelbase. It comes with a 385-litre boot.

The fourth-gen Octavia features a 10-inch touchscreen infotainment system with wireless Android Auto and Apple CarPlay, a 12-speaker Canton sound system, connected car tech, ambient lighting, a digital driver’s display, cruise control, paddle shifters, dual-zone climate control, an air purifier, wireless phone charging, powered front seats, a powered tailgate, and keyless entry with push-button start. Passenger safety is taken care of by eight airbags, ABS with EBD, electronic stability control, electronic parking brake with auto-hold, tyre pressure monitor, park assist, and front and rear parking sensors with a rear view camera.

The Skoda Octavia’s only rival in the segment is the Hyundai Elantra.
