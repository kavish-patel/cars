JOY GLOBE

Joy e-bike Glob is an electric scooter available at a starting price of Rs. 72,492 in India. It is available in only 1 variant and 1 colour. Joy e-bike Glob generates 250 W power from its motor. Joy e-bike Glob comes up with both front and rear disc brakes.

The Glob is an electric scooter from Joy e-bike. The electric vehicle features a subtle design that comprises an apron mounted headlight, a single-tone paint with highlights of black, and round rear-view mirrors. The feature list on the electric scooter includes reverse assist, keyless operation, and LED lighting.

This electric scooter is powered by a 250W BLDC motor that is powered by a 60V/233AH battery pack. The top speed is rated at 25kmph while the company claims a range of 60km per full charge.
