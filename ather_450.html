ATHER 450X

The Ather 450X uses a 6kW 26Nm Permanent Magnet Synchronous (PMS) Motor linked to a new 21, 700 cell setup in the lithium-ion battery pack and Battery Management System (BMS). It offers a claimed range of 70km in Ride mode and 85km in Eco. The Ather 450X can accelerate from 0-40kmph in 3.41 seconds in Warp mode.

450X can go upto 116 km/charge after a full charge.

450X battery takes 5.45 Hours to get charged.

Ather currently offers the 450 in two variants: 450 Plus and 450X. The Plus variant offers a maximum range of 70km while the 450X gets 85km. The 450 Plus makes 5.4kW peak power and 22Nm of torque and the 450X develops 6kW and 26Nm. Last but not the least, the Ather has updated the bikes with Bluetooth connected call alerts and music control only for the 450X.
