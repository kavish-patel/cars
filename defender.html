LAND_ROVER_DEFENDER

Land Rover unveiled the new generation Defender SUV at the 2019 Frankfurt Motor Show. The model is offered in two body styles that include 90 and 110 wheelbase formats. The model was later introduced in India in February 2020, with prices starting at Rs 69.99 lakh (ex-showroom).

The new Land Rover Defender is currently priced between 73.98 lakh and Rs 1.08 crore (ex-showroom). The model is available in nine trims that include Standard, S, SE, HSE, X-Dynamic S, X-Dynamic SE, H-Dynamic HSE, First Edition and X.

Powertrain options on the 2021 Land Rover Defender include a 2.0-litre petrol engine and a 3.0-litre petrol engine. The former is capable of producing 296bhp and 400Nm of torque, while the latter produces 394bhp and 550Nm of torque. Also on offer is a 3.0-litre diesel motor that is tuned to produce 296bhp and 650Nm of torque. An eight-speed automatic transmission sends power to all four wheels via the AWD system.

Feature highlights of the Land Rover Defender include square-shaped LED headlamps, a panoramic sunroof, LED tail lights, 18-inch five-spoke alloy wheels, a 10-inch TouchPro infotainment system with Apple CarPlay and Android Auto, 360-degree camera, cruise control, and electronic air suspension. The brand also offers a range of customisation options such as the Explorer Pack, Adventure Pack, Country Pack, and the Urban Pack.

Land Rover Defender price starts at ₹ 80.72 Lakh and goes upto ₹ 1.22 Crore. The price of Petrol version for Defender ranges between ₹ 80.72 Lakh - ₹ 1.22 Crore and the price of Diesel version for Defender ranges between ₹ 1.04 Crore - ₹ 1.20 Crore.
