CITRON C5 AiRCROSS

The Citroën C5 Aircross is a compact crossover SUV produced by French manufacturer Citroën since 2017.

It started as the Citroën Aircross concept car which was unveiled at the 2015 Shanghai Auto Show.

The production version was officially presented for the Chinese market at the 2017 Shanghai Auto Show.
