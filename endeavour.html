FORD ENDEAVOUR

Ford has announced that it will stop production of all its cars in India. That said, the current lineup will be on sale till stocks last and the company will continue to offer support to existing Ford car owners.

Ford retails the full-size SUV from Rs 29.99 lakh to Rs 36.25 lakh (ex-showroom Delhi).

It is available in four variants Titanium AT, Titanium+ AT 4x2, Titanium+ AT 4x4, and Sport AT.

The Endeavour can seat up to seven occupants.

Ford has equipped the Endeavour with a 2.0-litre turbo-diesel unit (170PS/420Nm), paired with a 10-speed AT (a first in India). There is no manual transmission on offer. It comes with 2WD and 4WD drivetrain options.

It gets FordPass connected car technology as standard, allowing you to perform remote vehicle operations, track live location, and get an overview of the carâ€™s telematics via the smartphone app. Existing features include active noise cancellation for the cabin, semi-autonomous parallel park assist, power-folding third-row seats, dual-zone climate control, and a panoramic sunroof. It also gets an 8-inch SYNC 3 touchscreen infotainment system with Apple CarPlay and Android Auto connectivity.

Safety features on offer include seven airbags, tyre pressure monitoring, and hill launch assist.

The Endeavour fights it out with the MG Gloster, Mahindra Alturas G4, Volkswagen Tiguan Allspace, Toyota Fortuner, and the 2021 Skoda Kodiaq.
